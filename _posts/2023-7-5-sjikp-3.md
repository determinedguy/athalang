## Things

### Pytanie

- Co to jest? (What is this?)
- Kto to jest? (Who is this?)
- Czy to jest...? (Is this...?)

### Odpowiedź

- To jest... (This is..., Pojedynczy/Singular)
- To są... (These are..., Mnogi/Plural)
- Tak, to jest... (Yes, this is...)
- Nie, to nie jest... To jest... (No, this is not... This is...)
